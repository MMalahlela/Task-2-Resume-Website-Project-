About Us

At PAWsitive Change, our mission is to provide a safe haven for animals in need, promote kindness and compassion, and advocate for their welfare. We believe every animal deserves a loving home and a second chance at a happy life.

Our Vision

We envision a world where no animal suffers from neglect, abuse, or abandonment, and where every pet has a safe, loving forever home.

Our Programs

  • Rescue & Rehabilitation: Our dedicated team rescues animals from crisis situations, providing medical care, shelter, and love.
  • Adoption Services: We match animals with forever families, ensuring a smooth transition and lifelong bond.
  • Community Outreach: We educate the public about animal welfare, promoting empathy and responsible pet ownership.
  • Animal Shelter Support: We partner with local shelters, providing resources and support to care for animals in need.
  • Therapy Animal Program: Our trained therapy animals bring joy and comfort to those in need.
